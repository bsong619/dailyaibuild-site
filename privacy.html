★ Daily AI Build

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

Daily AI Build is a personal-use application that posts content to a single TikTok account (@dailyaibuild) on behalf of its operator, Brenden Song. This policy explains what TikTok data the application accesses and how it is handled.

What we collect

  • TikTok OAuth tokens for the account owner only. Stored locally on the operator's machine in an encrypted-at-rest .env file. Never transmitted to any third party.
  • Account basic info returned by TikTok's user.info.basic scope (open ID, display name) — used solely to confirm the connected account.
  • Upload status returned by TikTok's Content Posting API endpoints — used to confirm publishes succeeded.

What we do with it

  • Authenticate the operator's TikTok account via TikTok Login Kit (PKCE OAuth flow).
  • Upload locally-rendered videos created by the operator to the operator's own TikTok account via the Content Posting API.
  • Set the AI-generated content flag on every upload (videos contain a synthesized voice clone of the operator and programmatically generated visuals).

What we don't do

  • Collect data from any TikTok user other than the operator's own account.
  • Share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties.
  • Store data anywhere outside the operator's local machine.
  • Use TikTok data to train models or for any purpose other than publishing the operator's own content.

Data retention

OAuth tokens are stored locally only and can be revoked by the operator at any time via TikTok's account settings. Logs and rendered videos are kept locally for the operator's own reference; they contain no data about other TikTok users.

Third-party services

The application calls the TikTok Open API. TikTok's privacy practices are governed by their own Privacy Policy. Voice synthesis is provided by ElevenLabs and only processes operator-supplied scripts (no user-identifying data sent to ElevenLabs).

Children

This application is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from anyone other than the operator.

Changes

Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
