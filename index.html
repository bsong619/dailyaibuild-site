★ Daily AI Build

Voice-to-video for one creator.

A personal pipeline that turns short scripts into vertical videos and publishes them to @dailyaibuild on TikTok.

What it does

Daily AI Build is a personal-use application operated by Brenden Song. It takes a written script, synthesizes a voiceover (using an ElevenLabs voice clone of the operator), renders animated visuals via Remotion, and uploads the resulting video to the operator's own TikTok account.

Who uses it

Only the operator. This application is not offered as a service to anyone else and only authenticates against a single TikTok account: @dailyaibuild.

What it accesses

  • user.info.basic — to confirm the connected account.
  • video.upload — to upload rendered video files.
  • video.publish — to publish those uploads to the operator's TikTok account.

Synthetic media disclosure

Voice is an ElevenLabs cloned voice of the operator (with the operator's consent — it is his own voice). Visuals are programmatically generated. Every upload sets TikTok's AI-generated content flag.

Open source

Source code is available at github.com/bsong619/ai-build-channel.
